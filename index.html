MT Specs, Features and Price

The Yamaha MT 15 is powered by a 155 cc air-cooled engine which produces 18.4 PS @ 10000 rpm of power. It has a fuel tank of 10 L and a claimed mileage of 56.87 kmpl. The Yamaha MT 15 starts at Rs 1.67 and goes up to Rs 1.73 Lakh. It is available in three variants.
